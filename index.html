Welcome to Afghan Proverbs

Afghan proverbs reflect the wisdom, humor, and culture of our people. Handed down from one generation to another, these sayings teach life lessons with poetic simplicity and deep meaning.

Feature Proverb

صبر تلخ است ولی میوه‌اش شیرین است

Patience is bitter, but its fruit is sweet.

تا مرد سخن نگفته باشد، عیب و هنرش نهفته باشد

Until a man speaks, his flaws and virtues remain hidden.

Proverb Gallery

هر که بامش بیش، برفش بیشتر

The higher the roof, the more snow it gathers.

More power brings more responsibility.

آب که یک جا بماند، می‌گندد

Water that stays in one place, rots.

Without motion, life decays.

گدایی که سرافراز است، پادشاه است

A proud beggar is a king.

Dignity is more valuable than wealth.

نخند که بلایت نیامده

Don’t laugh until misfortune hits you.

Do not mock others—you may face the same.

Proverb of the Day

هر که بامش بیش، برفش بیشتر

The higher the roof, the more snow it gathers.

This proverb teaches us that with more wealth, power, or status comes greater responsibility and stress. It reminds us not to envy others blindly and to be aware of the hidden burdens success might carry.

About

This website is a student project created to celebrate the rich oral tradition of Afghanistan through proverbs. It aims to showcase the timeless wisdom found in our everyday expressions, keeping the culture alive for future generations.

Afifa Ghaznawi is a passionate advocate for Afghan culture and education. As a native of Afghanistan, Afifa is dedicated to preserving Afghan traditions and sharing them with the world. She is a multilingual educator, fluent in English, Persian, Urdu, and Turkish. Afifa has been helping students both inside Afghanistan and abroad, especially Afghan girls, to achieve their goals despite the challenges they face.

Afifa is also involved in various projects, including offering private English lessons and contributing to educational initiatives for underprivileged communities. Her background in teaching and commitment to empowering others are reflected in the content of this website.
